Eli Shane leads a team of Slugslinging heroes 
to defeat evil forces in a deep underground world. 
They collect, train, and duel with SLUGS, who 
transform into powerful magical beasts. The story 
follows Eli and his crew as they explore caverns, 
challenge rivals, and upgrade gear. However, Dr. Blakk, 
a ghoul, threatens the world and the slugs, transforming 
them into mindless weapons.
